Key Insights & Opportunities

Strengths

  • Strong educational ecosystem with University of Mississippi
  • Above-average healthcare worker concentration
  • Cultural amenities and university town vitality
  • Positive net migration trends

Opportunities

  • Improve student retention to combat brain drain
  • Enhance infrastructure investment for economic diversification
  • Expand broadband access for rural areas
  • Develop more tech industry presence

Action Items

  • Create entrepreneur-in-residence programs
  • Improve public transportation connectivity
  • Develop affordable housing initiatives
  • Strengthen university-community partnerships
